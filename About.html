About SCO Theater Productions

At SCO Theater Production, we are more than just a non-equity theatre company — we are a collective of artists driven by passion, creativity, and a shared love of performance. Here, actors, directors, designers, writers, and theatre enthusiasts come together to create meaningful, engaging, and unforgettable productions.

Our mission is simple: to provide a space where talent is nurtured, new ideas are explored, and artistry is celebrated. Whether you’re stepping onto the stage for the first time or bringing years of experience, SCO Theater Production values commitment, collaboration, and a deep respect for the craft.

For us, theatre is not just about entertainment — it’s about storytelling, emotion, discipline, teamwork, and self-expression. Every rehearsal, every scene, every performance is an opportunity to grow, connect, and create something extraordinary together.

What We Stand For:

  • A supportive and inclusive creative environment
  • Uncompromising professionalism and dedication to quality
  • Artistic growth, learning, and development
  • Teamwork, respect, and reliability
  • Bringing powerful, transformative stories to life

Whether you're here to audition, perform, work behind the scenes, or simply support the journey, you are an essential part of this company.

This is where creativity meets commitment.

This is where performers become storytellers.

This is where productions become experiences.

Welcome to SCO Theater Production — Let’s create something unforgettable.
